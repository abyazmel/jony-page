JONY

Azerbaijani-born Russian pop singer & songwriter

Biography

Born Jahid Afrail oglu Huseynli (February 29, 1996) in Baku, Azerbaijan, JONY moved to Moscow at age 4.

He gained major attention with his single “Kometa” released in 2019.

JONY is known for his blend of pop, hookah rap, and R&B styles and has won multiple awards in Russia.
